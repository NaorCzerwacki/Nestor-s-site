Hello, my name is Joseph Nestor Czerwacki, and am a quality assurance adviser.

I work as a quality freelancer auditor on behalf of Sibat (Israel Foreign Defense Assistance and Export Organization) and the DCMA (the US Defense Contract Management Agency), when my job is to review and approve export of Israeli made products into US military units such as US Army, US Navy, USAF and US NATO forces.

In the last 28 years, I worked as director of quality assurance in a number of different industries, including the food industry, chemical industry and metal industry.
I am highly experienced and in contact with companies which authorized and confirmed the suitability of national and international standards, both local and foreign, such as ISO 9001-2008, TFMS, BRC, P2P, TN, AS9100, ISO 17025, SII 005, Global Gap and more.
I combine quality assurance with developing systems for TQM, statistical methods based on SPC such as DEMING and JURAN which help optimizing the systems by introducing computerized applications as per order, self-development and adjustment which include cost reduction.

Also, I monitor processes and help creating a "Brain Storm" in the work place, in order to integrate solutions through learning and mutual help and building a system MRB. As a part of my work I also guide various teams and help operating quality teams on topics which relate to the law and committed on the basis of which, such as staff safety, staff HACCP (food safety) and other.
